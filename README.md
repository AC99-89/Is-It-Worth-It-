Is It Worth It?
See what your subscriptions cost in work, not dollars.
What it does
Most people think about subscriptions in dollars. This app reframes them in something more visceral — your time. Enter your hourly wage, add your recurring subscriptions, and see exactly how many hours and workdays you spend each year just keeping them alive.
Features

Step-by-step onboarding — enter your wage first, then add subscriptions one by one
Per-subscription breakdown — hours per year, days per year, and a blunt verdict for each charge
Combined total — see your full subscription drag at a glance
National average comparison — see how you stack up against the average American's $924/year subscription spend
2,000-hour work year bar — visualize what slice of your working year goes to subscriptions vs. time you actually keep
Shareable receipt — a clean, screenshot-ready card showing your full subscription stack
Local persistence — your expenses are saved between sessions
